> A curated list of resources, organizations, tools, and readings focused on digital rights, freedom of expression, privacy, access to information, and internet governance.

Digital rights are human rights in the digital age. This list is intended for activists, developers, researchers, policymakers, and anyone who values digital freedom and online civil liberties.

## General Resources

- [Digital Rights Watch](https://digitalrightswatch.org.au/) – Australian nonprofit advocating for digital civil rights.
- [Ranking Digital Rights](https://rankingdigitalrights.org/) – Evaluates tech companies on privacy, expression, and transparency.
- [Access Now](https://www.accessnow.org/) – Defends and extends the digital rights of users at risk.

## Organizations & Advocacy

- [Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF)](https://www.eff.org/) – Leading nonprofit defending civil liberties in the digital world.
- [Article 19](https://www.article19.org/) – Global organization promoting freedom of expression and information.
- [Privacy International](https://privacyinternational.org/) – Advocates for strong data protection and privacy globally.
- [Center for Democracy & Technology (CDT)](https://cdt.org/) – Policy advocacy for democratic values in the digital age.
- [Open Rights Group](https://www.openrightsgroup.org/) – UK-based advocacy group focused on digital rights.

## Legal & Policy Resources

- [EPIC – Electronic Privacy Information Center](https://epic.org/) – Research and advocacy on privacy and civil liberties.
- [Stanford Center for Internet and Society](https://cyberlaw.stanford.edu/) – Research on internet law and policy.
- [Internet Law and Policy Foundry](https://www.ilpfoundry.us/) – Community for early-career internet law and policy professionals.

## Access to Information

- [Open Knowledge Foundation](https://okfn.org/) – Promotes open data and access to information.
- [The Right to Information (RTI) Rating](https://www.rti-rating.org/) – Global index comparing access-to-information laws.
- [FreedomInfo.org](http://www.freedominfo.org/) – International resource on freedom of information.

## Freedom of Expression

- [Global Freedom of Expression – Columbia University](https://globalfreedomofexpression.columbia.edu/) – Legal cases and analysis on free speech.
- [IFEX](https://ifex.org/) – Global network defending free expression and press freedom.
- [Freedom House – Freedom on the Net](https://freedomhouse.org/report/freedom-net) – Annual reports on internet freedom.

## Surveillance & Censorship

- [Surveillance Self-Defense (EFF)](https://ssd.eff.org/) – Tips for protecting yourself from surveillance.
- [The Citizen Lab](https://citizenlab.ca/) – Research on digital espionage, censorship, and surveillance.
- [NetBlocks](https://netblocks.org/) – Tracks internet disruptions and censorship in real time.
- [GreatFire](https://en.greatfire.org/) – Monitoring and testing internet censorship in China.

## Internet Governance

- [Internet Governance Forum (IGF)](https://www.intgovforum.org/) – UN platform for multi-stakeholder dialogue.
- [ICANN](https://www.icann.org/) – Coordinates the global domain name system (DNS).
- [Mozilla Internet Health Report](https://foundation.mozilla.org/en/internet-health-report/) – Explores global internet issues.

## Privacy & Security Tools

- [PrivacyTools.io](https://www.privacytools.io/) – Tools to protect your online privacy.
- [Tor Project](https://www.torproject.org/) – Free software and open network for anonymity.
- [Tails OS](https://tails.boum.org/) – Live OS focused on preserving privacy and anonymity.

## Guides & Learning

- [Security in a Box](https://securityinabox.org/en/) – Digital security tools and tactics for activists.
- [Cybersecurity for Journalists (CPJ)](https://cpj.org/resources/digital-security/) – Safety resources for media professionals.
- [Digital Security Helpline (Access Now)](https://www.accessnow.org/help/) – 24/7 assistance for civil society at risk.

## Reports & Research

- [UN Special Rapporteur on Freedom of Expression](https://www.ohchr.org/en/special-procedures/sr-freedom-of-expression) – UN body reporting on global free expression.
- [Reset Tech](https://reset.tech/) – Research and policy recommendations on tech and democracy.
- [Tech and Human Rights Resource Hub (Human Rights Watch)](https://www.hrw.org/topic/technology-and-rights) – Reporting and analysis on tech’s impact on rights.
